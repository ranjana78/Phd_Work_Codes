Medical Image Datasets – Official Download Links
This document provides official and reliable download links for commonly used dermatology and skin disease image datasets, suitable for research, benchmarking, and academic use.
________________________________________
1. ISIC 2018 Dataset (Skin Lesion Analysis)
Description:
The ISIC 2018 Challenge dataset is widely used for tasks such as skin lesion classification, segmentation, and attribute detection. It contains dermoscopic images with expert annotations.
Official Source:
International Skin Imaging Collaboration (ISIC)
Download Link:
https://challenge.isic-archive.com/data/#2018
Notes: - Requires free account registration and login - Images provided with ground-truth labels - Commonly used in MICCAI and CVPR research
________________________________________
2. Derm7pt Dataset
Description:
Derm7pt is a dermatology dataset designed around the 7-point checklist for melanoma detection. It includes clinical images, dermoscopic images, and structured diagnostic criteria.
Official Source:
Medical University of Vienna
Download Link:
https://derm.cs.sfu.ca/Welcome.html
Alternative Mirror (Zenodo):
https://zenodo.org/record/3567755
Notes: - Freely available for academic research - Includes meta-data and diagnostic criteria - Suitable for explainable AI and few-shot learning research
________________________________________
3. SD-198 (Skin Disease-198) Dataset
Description:
SD-198 contains 6,584 clinical images across 198 different skin disease categories, making it useful for fine-grained classification and long-tailed learning.
Official Source:
Originally released by Chinese research groups
Download Link:
https://github.com/xpwu95/SD-198
Direct Baidu Cloud Link (as provided by authors):
https://pan.baidu.com/s/1rF6G6N8mF0ZAxk2nZc6P0Q
Extraction Code:
Provided on the GitHub page
Notes: - Registration not required - Some labels may require translation/cleaning - Widely used for multi-class dermatology benchmarks
